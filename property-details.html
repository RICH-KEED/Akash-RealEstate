Description

This breathtaking beachfront villa offers the ultimate luxury lifestyle with direct access to pristine white sand beaches and panoramic ocean views from nearly every room.

Meticulously designed with high-end finishes throughout, this contemporary masterpiece features soaring ceilings, floor-to-ceiling windows, and an open floor plan perfect for entertaining. The gourmet kitchen boasts custom cabinetry, top-of-the-line appliances, and a large center island.

The lavish primary suite includes a private terrace, stunning ocean views, a spa-like bathroom with a freestanding tub, and dual walk-in closets. Four additional en-suite bedrooms provide luxurious accommodations for family and guests.

Outdoor living spaces include an infinity pool that appears to merge with the ocean horizon, a summer kitchen, multiple lounging and dining areas, and lush tropical landscaping for privacy.

Additional features include smart home technology, a home theater, wine cellar, fitness room, elevator, and a three-car garage. This exceptional property offers the perfect balance of luxury, privacy, and beachfront living.
